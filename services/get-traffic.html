SERVICE 02

SEO / GEO / AEO

Next-generation search optimization for traditional search, local discovery, and AI-powered answers.

What it is

SEO is the foundation — making sure Google ranks you. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the next layer — ensuring AI search engines like Google SGE and ChatGPT cite your business. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) structures your content so machines can extract and surface your answers. Together, these three pillars make your business visible everywhere people search in 2026.

Who it's for

Any business that relies on being found online. If your competitors are showing up in search results and AI overviews but you're nowhere, you're losing business every single day without knowing it.

What Orrion does differently

Most agencies still treat SEO like it's 2018 — keyword stuffing, generic blog posts, and backlink spam. We build search strategies for the AI era. That means structured data, entity-based optimization, content that answers real questions, and technical foundations that make your site the source both Google and AI models trust. We don't chase algorithm tricks. We build search authority that compounds over time.
